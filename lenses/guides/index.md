Per-lens teaching guides (motivating question, intuition, mini-model, worked-example sketch)
used when a lens runs Teach → Model → Practice (`../../modes/lens.md`).

The detailed guide content currently lives at
[`../../resources/heuristics/_lens-guides.md`](../../resources/heuristics/_lens-guides.md),
with full technique reference in `../../resources/heuristics/0{1-5}-*.md`. Specialist skills
load those paths directly.

> is the Lens Library's entry point into that content; keep one source of truth — add new lens
> guidance to `_lens-guides.md`, not here.
