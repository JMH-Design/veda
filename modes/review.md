# REVIEW — revisit and retrieve previous models

> **Mode.** Spaced-retrieval pass over models the learner already built. The Tutor Loop
> (`../core/tutor-loop.md`) weighted toward Diagnose → Retrieve → Feedback.

**Use when:** the learner wants to refresh prior models, or a prediction/misconception review
date has arrived.

**Old name:** optional/new in this redesign.

---

## What REVIEW does

Keeps durable models durable. Pulls a Mastery Card (or a flagged misconception, or a decision
memo at its review date) and asks the learner to **retrieve before re-reading**.

---

## Flow

1. **Pick the target.** A Mastery Card from `MEMORY.md`, a `MISCONCEPTIONS.md` row flagged
   "review later," or a `DECIDE` memo at its prediction date.
2. **Retrieve cold.** "Before we look — rebuild the model. What are the moving parts?"
3. **Compare.** Reveal the card; learner checks their reconstruction against it.
4. **Feedback** (`../core/feedback-protocol.md`) — note what faded, what held.
5. **One transfer rep** to re-anchor it (drop into PRACTICE if more reps help).
6. **For decision reviews:** did the prediction hold? What does that teach? Update the memo and,
   if a model needs revising, the EVD lens.

---

## Capture

- Update the `LEARNER.md` mastery-map row (confidence, last practice, next rep).
- Resolve or re-flag the `MISCONCEPTIONS.md` row.
- For decisions: record the outcome vs. prediction in the memo; update `MEMORY.md` if the model changed.

---

## Anti-patterns

- Re-reading the card *to* the learner instead of having them retrieve first
- Reviewing everything at once — pick what's actually due
- Skipping the "did the prediction hold?" check on decision reviews
